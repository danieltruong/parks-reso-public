Passes can be booked starting at 7:00am, two days in advance of your planned visit.

  • Passes will remain available until capacity is met — additional passes may be released each day at 7:00am, dependent on weather conditions.
  • Passes cannot be transferred to another date, time, or location and your pass cannot be shared beyond the group reserved.
  • 1 pass is required per vehicle - Maximum of 8 persons (adult or child) per pass per day.

Maximum of one A.M. and one P.M. vehicle pass can be booked per day/per person.

  • A.M. pass permits parking from 7:00am to noon.
  • P.M. pass starts at noon and reserves a spot until 4:00pm.
  • After 4:00pm, day-use parking spots will be claimed on a first come, first serve basis.
  • To reserve a full day visit - please book both an A.M. and P.M. pass consecutively for the date you would like to attend.
